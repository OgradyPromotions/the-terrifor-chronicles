The Chronicles of the Terrifor

Imagination, unity, and culture vs. the shadows of silence.

The Chronicles of the Terrifor is a global children’s adventure series where courage, creativity, and individuality stand against a mysterious force that thrives on silence and conformity. In every corner of the world, a different Terrifor rises, feeding on the culture, music, language, and spirit of a place. But in each story, a young hero discovers that imagination and unity are stronger than fear. From the coulees of Lethbridge to Stockholm, Sweden, all the way to Tairāwhiti (Gisborne) New Zealand, ordinary kids rise up to protect the soul of their communities.

Blending myth, mystery, and real‑world locations, The Chronicles of the Terrifor celebrates the power of culture and the beauty of being different. Each story invites readers into a new place, a new hero, and a new battle to keep creativity alive. Because when people dare to express who they truly are, even the darkest shadows cannot silence the world.
